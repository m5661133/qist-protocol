> ## What is Qist?
>
> **Qist (قسط)** is the first Islamic decentralized Murabaha finance platform built on Base blockchain. It enables halal installment purchases of ETH and BTC — no bank, no intermediary, no riba (interest).
>
> **قسط** هي أول منصة مرابحة إسلامية لا مركزية على بلوكشين Base. تتيح شراء ETH وBTC بالتقسيط الحلال — بدون بنك، بدون وسيط، بدون ربا.
>
> ---
>
> ## How It Works
>
> 1. **Seller** lists an asset (ETH/BTC) for Murabaha sale with profit margin
> 2. 2. **Buyer** purchases via smart contract with USDC collateral
>    3. 3. Smart contract enforces Sharia-compliant payment schedule
>       4. 4. Asset transfers directly to buyer on full payment
>         
>          5. ---
>         
>          6. ## Key Features
>         
>          7. - ✅ **Halal** — fully Sharia-compliant Murabaha structure
> - ✅ **Decentralized** — smart contracts on Base Mainnet (no intermediary)
> - - ✅ **Transparent** — all transactions on-chain and auditable
>   - - ✅ **No Riba** — profit is fixed at contract creation (no floating interest)
>     - - ✅ **Grace Period** — 3-day grace period before liquidation (رفق بالمدين)
